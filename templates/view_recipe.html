My Favorite Crepes

Location: Paris, France by Josh Ackerman

Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 lemon
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp honey

Directions

1. Mix all of the ingredients together except for the butter
2. Heat up a nonstick pan
3. Add a little bit of the butter and spread around
4. Make crepes like normal
